Why HAWAII?

Hawaii's activity and nature: Hawaii's beaches, culture, and beauty transcends history and people. 
"Travelling to Hawaii provides an ability to experience different culture and love for nature" 

Hawaii islands has around 137 islands with 4 majors ones. Those are the Hawaii Island (Big Island), Maui, Oahu, Kanai, Lanai, Molokai, Niihau, and Kahoolawe.

It has around 6400 sq. Miles and 1.4 million people living in it. Out of which almost 2/3 of the population reside in Oahu island.

Hawaii was first settled by Polynesians between 300-600AD. It then became a US. Territory in 1900 and 50th US State in August 21st 1959. The state capital is Honolulu which is located in Oahu Island.


  1. CULTURE The culture of Hawaii is about 1500 years and filled with history. Along with history, it’s filled fascinating traditions, values, legends, and customs. The culture has its origins from the Polynesians and based around traditional gods and goddesses. It includes: Pele (a wicked volcano goddess), Poliahu (a snow god), and Maui (a demi-god).

    Hula: Hula signifies one of Hawaii’s key cultural icon. It includes people in colourful costumes and grass skirts, who dance with graceful hand movements and its signature swaying hips.

    Religion: The traditional Hawaiian religion is polytheistic animistic religion where Hawaiians believe there are spirits. They believe in 4 main gods: Kāne (God of creation), Kanaloa (God of ocean), Ku (God of war), and Lono (God of peace). They also believe in ka hā (40 male gods), ke kanahā (400 gods and goddesses), na ‘unihipili (spirits) and na ‘aumākua (guardians).

    Music: It is an important part of culture and has a unique mix of many influences that celebrate the island life. The key symbols of Hawaii’s musical culture are ukulele, and reggae to slack-key and steel guitar, falsetto and “Jawaiian”.

  2. ACTIVITY Whether interested in surfing, or simply sightseeing, there is an activity for everyone.

    Sightseeing: The beautiful mountain ranges, deep blue oceans, diverse landscapes makes it a beautiful place to sight see in the Hawaii.

    Helicopter Tours: To see the natural beautiful at a glance, the helicopter tours allows to see Hawaii with a bird’s eye view.

    Snorkelling: Snorkelling allows visitors to see and learn about the diverse and colourful marine life that exists under the ocean.

    Luau: Luau is Hawaiian and Polynesian style luau feasts. It allows visitors to learn about Hawaiian arts like basket weaving and also partake in the culture. Cutural presentations like Hawaiian hula dances and fire dance performances immerses people in its history and culture.

    Surfing: Surfing is a key activity of Hawaii. The waves of the island still keep Hawaii as one of the best places to surf in the whole world.

  3. ENVIRONMENT   Whether it's beach, forest or mountains, the beauty can be all round. Hawaii's beautiful mountains and beaches make it one of the most famous travel destinations.           

    Climate: In Hawaiian islands, the weather is diverse but tropical climate is most common. This allows visitors to enjoy the warm weather and its beaches. For rainfall, Hawaii receives its most precipitation from October to April. From May to September, it’s much drier weather. Temperatures can also differ for the islands The sea level varies from 26 to 31C, but higher up the temperature lowers. It can even have snow in summits of Mauna Kea, Mauna Loa during winter time.
